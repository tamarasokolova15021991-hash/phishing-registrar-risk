Legal Notice & Data Sources

This report contains factual statistics only. It does not allege intentional wrongdoing, criminal activity, or complicity by any registrar. Large registrars naturally process more domain registrations and may appear higher in volume-based rankings. Risk Score is normalized to reflect abuse rate relative to peers.

Data collection: Domains are identified through BrandProtect.io's proprietary threat intelligence platform, which continuously monitors multiple independent threat intelligence networks, DNS telemetry, and OSINT feeds. Every domain is independently verified by an ensemble of AI models (confidence threshold ≥70%) before inclusion.

Right of correction: Any registrar that believes their data is inaccurate may contact research@brandprotect.io with supporting evidence. Verified corrections will be applied within 5 business days.

Industry precedent: Spamhaus Registrar Intelligence, ICANN Monthly Registrar Reports, Netcraft Phishing Reports — establishing precedent for this category of security research.
